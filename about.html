I'm a data geek. I like to measure things that are hard to measure. Bonus points for things nobody else has thought to measure.

I've measured:

  • The impact of government subsidy programs on agricultural production - after the structure of the programs was changed to remove any impact on production. The results are here .

  • The impact on energy company stock prices when the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission changes it's auction rules. The results were published with Lon Peters here .

  • The federal government's overpayments to Medicare and Medicaid service providers. Cases I've worked on have recovered nearly a billion dollars for the federal and state governments. Examples of cases I contributed to include:

    • An undisclosed fraction of $3,000,000,000 from british drugmaker Glaxo-SmithKline for overcharging Medicare by bundling drugs to obfuscate their average market price.

    • $65,000,000 from the LHC group for performing unnecessary medical procedures.

    • $45,000,000 from Alpharma Inc. for illegally marketing morphine-based drugs to doctors.

    • $145,000,000 from Sandoz and Watson for overcharging the government for prescription drugs.

    • $154,000,000 from Par Pharmaceuticals for overcharging the government for prescription drugs
